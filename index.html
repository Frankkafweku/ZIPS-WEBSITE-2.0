About ZIPS CBU

The Zambian Institute of Purchasing and Supply (ZIPS) - CBU Chapter was formed to unite students pursuing studies in purchasing and supply. Joining the chapter provides valuable networking opportunities, access to academic resources, and exposure to professional practices that prepare members for successful careers in supply chain management.
